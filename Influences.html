WorldGrower Influences


D&D

Dungeons&Dragons is an obvious influence because of the 6 character stats:
Strength, Constitution, Dexterity, Intelligence, Wisdom and Charisma.

Many spells like enlarge, reduce, disguise self, minor illusion, mend, etc all find their roots in D&D.


Elder scrolls


The Elder Scrolls series have produced great games like Morrowind, Oblivion and Skyrim.
The skill system in which a skill improves the more you use it is the same as in the Elder Scrolls.

Using energy to cast spells is another aspect of the Elder Scrolls.
It's an open-ended sandbox world in which the player is free to choose what to do.

The equipment slots and equipment that gets damaged are from Morrowind.

The Sims


The Sims series is responsible for the energy, food and water stats.
Many dialogue options are inspired by The Sims:
For example: diplomacy options like flattery, kissing, having sex, talking about subjects, etc.

The concept of relationships and family also finds its roots in The Sims.
